Athlete FAQ

How is this different from standard S&C?

Standard Strength & Conditioning often follows a generic template. At PRJCT: LEGACY, we integrate Exercise Physiology, meaning we specifically account for your injury history, biomechanical discrepancies, and physiological profile.

Do I need to be injured to start?

No. While we specialize in return-to-play, the majority of our athletes train with us to prevent injury and optimize their current performance output.

Do you work with all sports?

Yes. The principles of human physiology and force production apply across all sporting disciplines. We adapt the specific training variables to meet the energy system and mechanical demands of your sport.
