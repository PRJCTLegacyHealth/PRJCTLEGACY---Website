What is an Exercise Physiologist?

Many confuse Exercise Physiology (EP) with Physiotherapy. While we work together, our roles are distinct. Think of Physio as the "mechanic" fixing the acute breakdown, and EP as the "engineer" optimising the machine for the long haul.
